My Philosophy

Everyone Is An Artist

Since I was young I have seen people develop their skills and have a keen eye for what they love. What I had a keen eye for was art and I have observed that everybody seemed to enjoy it as well.

I have taken their liking for my skill into a passion and turned what I have started with from little drawings on paper into full fledged professional works of art. Everybody seems to celebrate what I do now, but from what I see, I should celebrate from what I started off with. I started off as any other kid, I just drew because I liked to. I never thought I would turn what I liked to do into something that I really wanted to do as a profession. Once I heard the encouragement of peers I started to draw more and more and it grew from there.

I would like to celebrate everybody's passion as well as the fact that everything takes practice to get good at. Everyone's passion is worth celebrating and cherishing, and once we all start cherishing and loving each others works the world will be a better place...
